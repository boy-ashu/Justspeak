🚀 About JustSpeak

JustSpeak is an innovative AI-powered platform designed to make human-computer interaction more natural, secure, and intelligent. Instead of typing commands, users can speak, interact, and feel understood.

🔄 How JustSpeak Works

  1. 1. Open the web app
  2. 2. Login with credentials or voice pattern
  3. 3. Speak your command naturally
  4. 4. Backend + AI processes your request instantly
  5. 5. Get intelligent voice or text response
